Buying Guide

How to Choose the Right Sofa Table Combination

Simple Buying Steps

  1. Measure your living room before buying furniture.
  2. Choose a sofa size that fits the room without blocking walkways.
  3. Select a coffee table that is proportional to the sofa length.
  4. Leave enough space between the sofa and table for comfortable movement.
  5. Match materials such as wood, metal, glass, fabric, or leather with your decor style.

Useful Size Tips

  • Coffee table height should be close to sofa seat height.
  • Leave around 14–18 inches between sofa and coffee table.
  • Side tables usually look best near sofa arm height.
  • Console tables should not be much taller than the sofa back.

Decor Advice

Helpful Sofa & Table Styling Tips

Balance Size and Shape

A large sectional sofa often works well with a rectangular or oversized coffee table, while smaller sofas may look better with round or oval tables.

Layer with Rugs and Lighting

Place your sofa and table on a rug to define the seating area. Add lamps or soft lighting for a warm and inviting atmosphere.

Keep Table Decor Practical

Use trays, books, candles, bowls, or plants, but leave enough empty space for daily use, drinks, snacks, and remotes.
